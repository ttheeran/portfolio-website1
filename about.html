About

My Story

I’ve spent the last 10+ years bridging creativity and technology—designing visuals for retail spaces, crafting graphics for film, supporting instructional designers, and experimenting with motion graphics. My career at Apple Retail sharpened my technical knowledge and people-first approach, teaching me how to guide users through complex problems with clarity and empathy. Now, I bring that same blend of design expertise and technical fluency into UX, creating digital products that are intuitive, beautiful, and built with real people in mind.

Outside of design, I’m passionate about staying active and inspired. I enjoy exploring the outdoors through hiking, keeping fit through regular training, and following the latest tech trends that shape how we live and work. I’m also an avid fan of film and cinema, always finding new ways to draw creative inspiration from storytelling on screen.
